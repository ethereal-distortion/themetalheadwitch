Welcome to my wonderful world of witchcraft!

Hi! My name is Sera, and I'm a magical practicioner of over three years. I strive to, through an ever-expanding knowledge of witchcraft and the metaphysical, develop a craft for myself that is unique, personal, and undeniably magical.

The idea of practicing witchcraft is something that had called out to me since childhood. I can remember being no older than twelve or thirteen when I picked up a book on anything metaphysical for the first time and knowing in my heart that something about it just felt so right. For the next few years, on and off, I'd revisit witchcraft and paganism, never fully committing (for a number of reasons), though always keeping it in the back of my mind, almost as if I knew I was destined to completely turn to it one day. And, in late 2019, just months before the pandemic hit, I finally did... and I've never looked back.

Once I began to take my witchcraft more seriously, I quickly discovered the wealth of information (and misinformation) circulating concerning the metaphysical. That said, whilst I certainly don't think I know everything about witchcraft (in fact, I feel a good practicioner is perfectly aware that they'll always be learning more), I wanted to develop this website as a way to archive all that I do know for anyone and everyone who feels it may benefit their practice.

(NOTE: if one comes across information that is incorrect, please feel free to contact me via any of my social media platforms; I am beyond happy to make any necessary adjustments.)
