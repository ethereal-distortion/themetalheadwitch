Welcome to my wonderful world of witchcraft!

Hi! My name is Sera, and I'm a magical practicioner of over three years. I strive to, through an ever-expanding knowledge of witchcraft and the metaphysical, develop a craft for myself that is unique, personal, undeniably magical, and absolutely metal.

The idea of practicing witchcraft is something that had called out to me since I was little. I can remember being no older than twelve or thirteen when I picked up a book on anything metaphysical for the first time and knowing in my heart that something about it just felt so right. For the next few years, on and off, I'd revisit witchcraft and paganism, never fully committing (for a number of reasons), though always keeping it in the back of my mind, almost as if I knew I was destined to completely turn to it one day. And, in late 2019, just months before the pandemic hit, I finally did... and I've never looked back.

Once I began to take my witchcraft more seriously, I quickly discovered the wealth of information (and misinformation) circulating concerning the metaphysical. That said, whilst I certainly don't think I know everything about witchcraft (in fact, I feel a good practitioner is perfectly aware that they'll always be learning more), I wanted to develop this website as a way to archive all that I do know for anyone and everyone who feels it may benefit their practice.

If one comes across information that is incorrect, please feel free to contact me via any of my social media platforms; I am beyond happy to make any necessary adjustments.

Why 'The Metalhead Witch'?

Rock N' Roll & Heavy Metal music is the centerfold of my life and has been ever since childhood. I remember so clearly being in kindergarten and listening to bands like Guns N' Roses, AC/DC, Mötley Crüe, Led Zeppelin, Black Sabbath, Aerosmith, and far too many more to name here. More than anything else in the world, these artists were (and are) my heroes and greatest inspirations throughout all I do. This music has made me who I am and has given me more strength, comfort, and confidence than I could ever depict. Moreover, when I stepped into the world of witchcraft, I simply knew that the Rock N' Roll and Heavy Metal I love so dearly would be coming with me somehow.

And how could it not? After all, the association between Rock N' Roll and Heavy Metal music with things like witchcraft and magic, Satanism and Devil-worship, and generally evil/demonic forces is something that has existed since before the establishment of Rock N' Roll as an genre and has remained infamous throughout its, and therefore Heavy Metal's, entire lifespan thus far. Whilst the majority of such claims is a result of racism and religious fearmongering (as well as bands who certainly leaned into said rumors), I still find that, to a degree, there is merit to the fact that this music is magical.

To learn more about both the history of metal and magic together, click here.

That said, for me personally at least, there is no witchcraft without heavy metal and there is no heavy metal without witchcraft; the two are fundamentally connected. Furthermore, calling myself 'The Metalhead Witch' reinforces the idea of the inherent relationship between the two. Even more than that, though, I call myself as such because it's just who I am; a tride n' true metalhead and a proud practitioner of witchcraft that truly, truly believes in the pure power and majesty of them both.

What kinds of things can I learn here?

Lots! Like I said, I definitely don't know it all, but I want to include all that I can! From things like fundamental correspondences to magical history to DIY-spellcraft and more, I want this to (eventually) become one extensive digital grimoire of information. With that said, if there's a topic not on here that someone would like to be covered, feel free to contact me via any of my social media platforms!
